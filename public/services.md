# Services, Margo Hairstylist

All services are by appointment at the Snells Beach home studio.

- **Cuts & Styling** — women, men and kids. A cut shaped to suit you and to sit well as it grows out, finished with a proper blow wave.
- **Colour** — full colour, root touch-ups, foils and balayage, always weighed against the health of your hair.
- **Keratin & Straightening** — smoothing and keratin treatments that leave frizz behind and make everyday hair far more manageable.
- **Curly Hair** — curl-literate cutting and styling that works with your natural pattern instead of fighting it.
- **Treatments & Blow Waves** — deep hydration treatments and salon blow waves using the Angel en Provence organic range.

Pricing is friendly and by consultation, so you always know the cost before we start.
